Conclusion

For the future, we want to add more adoptable pets to our list,

beef up our donation pages, and add more donor information,

make the search bar useable, and add more pages to our navigation

bar to make it more navigational.

Overall, our team worked hard together and completed a ton of work

in a limited amount of time. I pearsonally learned alot about the

redesign process. It was importnat to be open to the opinions and

reasonings of our group, as well as taking user feed back into

consideration. I am glad to be able to use the knowledge I gained

in future projects and I thank my team for that.
